Tips for the Best Results
☀️
Good Lighting
Natural light near a window works best. Avoid harsh shadows or overhead lighting.
👔
Dress the Part
Wear what you'd wear to a professional meeting. Solid colors work better than patterns.
📐
Mix Angles
Take photos straight on, slightly left, slightly right. Variety gives us more to work with.
😊
Natural Expressions
Relax. A natural slight smile looks far more professional than a forced one.
FAQ
Will it actually look like me? +
Yes — the AI is trained on your actual selfies so the headshots will look like you, just in a professional setting. The more variety in your selfies, the better the results.
What if I don't like the results? +
Pro and Team packages include a free revision round. If you're not happy we'll regenerate with adjusted settings. Your satisfaction is the goal.
How do I send you my selfies? +
After you order, we'll send you a simple intake form with an upload link. You can upload directly from your phone or computer — no apps needed.
Can I use these on LinkedIn and my resume? +
Absolutely — that's exactly what they're designed for. You get full rights to use the images however you want professionally or personally.
What makes a good selfie for this? +
Good lighting, facing the camera, natural expression, and professional or semi-professional clothing. Check the tips section above for more guidance.
